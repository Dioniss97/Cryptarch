# Sprint Next

Determine the next sprint to work on and prepare to begin.

## Instructions
1. Read `docs/sprints/sprint-checklist.md`.

3. Pick the next pending sprint in order.

6. If the checklist is inconsistent with files in `docs/sprints/`, report the mismatch before proceeding.
